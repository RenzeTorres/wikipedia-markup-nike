Nike

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services.

History of Nike

Nike was founded on January 25, 1964, by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports. It officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The company takes its name from Nike, the Greek goddess of victory.

Notable Nike Products

Nike is known for its innovative products, including:

  • Nike Air Max
  • Air Jordan Sneakers
  • Nike Dri-FIT Apparel

Athletes Endorsed by Nike

Nike has endorsed numerous athletes, including:

  1. Kobe Bryant
  2. LeBron James
  3. Michael Jordan

Nike is a major player in the sports and athletic footwear and apparel industry, known for its iconic swoosh logo and innovative products.
